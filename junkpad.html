Junkpad

Got your next genius idea? Want to write down that book that got recommended to you? Just remembered you have to do laundry today? Write it down in Junkpad to remember it for later. Or forget about it. Junkpad won’t judge you.

Embrace the chaos

Not everything has to be neatly organised, embrace your chaotic stream of consciousness. All notes appear in the same list, sorted by last edited. This makes it easy to add notes without any fuss. Just type.

Put a pin on it

Some notes are created more equal than others. Pin the most important ones to get them in your face, and increase your chances of not forgetting them.

It’s ok to pick favourites

There could be treasures even in a dumpster. Mark your favourite notes and keep them in a separate list. The VIPs of the junkyard if you will.
